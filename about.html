Message From The Principal

Assalaam u Alaikum

Starting in the name of Allah, the most Beneficent and the most Merciful, I thank Allah (SWT) for giving us the Imaan and creating us in the Ummah of Prophet Mohammed (S.A.W). We are very fortunate to be in Pakistan – Pakistan enjoying all sorts of benefits including having an excellent educational system as well as having the freedom to practice our belief system

The importance of education in Islam can be understood through a verse emphasizing the importance of education even in the most critical times of war

In Surah Tawba, verse 122, Allah states that

“With all this, it is not desirable that all of the believers take the field (in time of war). From within every group in their midst, some shall refrain from going to war and shall devote themselves (instead) to acquiring a deeper knowledge of the Faith, and (thus be able to) teach their home-coming brethren, so that these (too) might guard themselves against evil”

Knowledge means power. Education is not only important for an individual but it also has a profound impact on the whole of the nation.

I wish to take this opportunity to thank all staff, parents/caregivers, the Governance members, donors and well-wishers who have contributed to the success of this institute.

May Allah (SWT) guide us onto the right path and shower his blessings on the entire humanity.

Fahad Ansari
Principal
